Dear Academic and Industrial Experts,

On behalf of Huawei, we would like to invite you to attend Huawei HISC (Hardware, Industrial, Security & Cryptography workshop) @CHES 2025.

We are committed to open innovation and working with our partners to make breakthroughs in basic research, and we spare no effort as we probe at the very frontiers of science and technology. As part of our efforts to broaden collaboration, we actively engage with global academic associations and strengthen collaboration with universities and research institutes around the world. We exchange ideas and share the problems we face, and work together to address our industry's toughest challenges, so as to drive technological progress for everyone.

HISC@CHES 2025 is a workshop focusing on major industry trends and technology directions on hardware security and cryptography. It is hosted by the Huawei Hardware Security Lab. At HISC, world leading researchers from academia, and top technical experts from industry, will come together to discuss the main industry trends and hot topics, share ideas on technology innovation and work together to push forward the development of industry.

HISC@CHES 2025 will take place on September 14th, in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. The theme of this workshop is Exchange - Enlighten - Explore.

The content of the workshop focused on the following topics:

  • Hardware Root of Trust
  • Cryptographic Engineering and Evaluation
  • Micro-architectural Secure Fuzzing
  • SoC Security Verification
  • PCB Tamper Protection
  • Device Supply Chain Validation
  • Hardware-based Anomaly Detection
  • Side Channel and Fault Injection Analysis

We sincerely invite you to participate in Huawei HISC@CHES 2025 and share your opinions about Hardware Security and Cryptography.
